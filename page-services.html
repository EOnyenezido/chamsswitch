Introducing Naira.com

A robust flexible comprehensive one-stop shop for all electronic payment and transaction services

Naira.com is Nigeria’s premier transaction portal which allows the provision of online transactional services ranging from bill payments, airtime top-up, ecommerce and lots more!

Key services offered through Naira.com include:

Payroll and Remittance

A real-time web based collection and remittance solution based on the standards set by NIBBS that facilitates single and bulk remittances and collection.

Airtime Top-up

Secure, convenient and fast online mobile recharge and airtime top up service that enables you top up your airtime anytime, anywhere, across all mobile networks.

Bill Payment

Make bill payment and subscription payments to several utility companies, sports companies or leisure clubs registerd with us; including DSTV, PHCN and lots more!

Payment Gateway

World class payment gateway that can process and settle payment transactions efficiently, using globally recognized solutions both locally and internationally.

Funds Transfer

Simple and convenient funds transfer module that enables both P2P and bulk transfer in a safe and secure enviroment across all recognized banks.

Online Commerce

Omni-channel, flexible and scalable e-commerce services from a single source. Smooth transaction to guarantee ultimate customer satisfaction.
