Who We Are

ChamsSwitch is a subsidiary of Chams Plc, an indigenous and a leading Information Technology solutions company with deep specialization in Card Technology and over 22 years of industry experience.

ChamsSwitch is essentially an electronic Payment Switch that has built a world class Transaction Switching platform that can process and settle payment transactions efficiently, using globally recognized solutions to aid interconnectivity to issuers, acquirers, merchants, service providers and other switches (both locally and internationally).

ChamsSwitch can enable you to accept and manage customer payments through multiple payment channels: online, by phone, using the internet and Point of sale (POS) terrminals.We consistently challenge ourselves to sustain a culture of excellence through well motivated and highly trainied people to respond proactiively to our customers’ needs and create superior returns to our shareholders whilst remaining a socially responsible company.

Vision

To be a world class company that provides the most convenient way to pay for any thing to any entity.
